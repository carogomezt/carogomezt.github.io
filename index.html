About Me

I'm from Colombia and am a student of System and Computer Engineering at Universidad Tecnologica de Pereira. I'll be graduating in 2017 and am an intelligent, persistent person, passionate about what I'm doing, especially front-end web development and competitive programming marathons. I work well in a team or independently, am goal-oriented, and have good listening and communication skills. I also like to share my knowledge with other people while learning more. For this reason, I'm part of a group called jointDeveloper that promotes gender equality and encourages women to get involved in technology areas. We share our knowledge through monthly talks and put into practice the knowledge we acquire.
